Career Compass

Not sure which career is right for you? Career Compass gives you clarity. We analyse your strengths, interests, and potential, then hand you a personalised roadmap so you know exactly where you're going and why.

  • 1-to-1 consultation call with your consultant
  • 3 matched career roles with 1 recommended path
  • Full learning path simulation, beginner to advanced

02

The Blueprint

You know your direction. Now it is time to build. The Blueprint gives you a fully structured learning plan, the resources to execute it, and dedicated guidance from someone invested in seeing you succeed. Your time, your pace, our support.

  • Full personalised learning path built around your target career
  • Courses and learning resources sourced, vetted and assigned on your behalf
  • Course and tools costs covered within your programme fee
  • Bi-weekly 1-to-1 sessions to review progress, address challenges and keep momentum
  • A project brief assigned by your consultant, completed independently and reviewed with detailed feedback
  • A closing mock interview or presentation session to assess your readiness
  • A written readiness report so you know exactly where you stand and what your next step should be
£750 one-time
Speak to Us
03

The Refinery

The Refinery is where potential becomes proof. A high-touch, cohort-based programme built around real accountability, genuine hand-holding and relentless confidence building. We do not just teach you. We prepare you for the room.

  • Courses sourced, vetted and assigned for your career path, with all course and resource costs included in your fee
  • Weekly hands-on project work that builds a portfolio employers can see and trust
  • A major capstone project that demonstrates the full depth of your capability
  • Weekly live sessions structured around active challenge and feedback, so rigour becomes second nature
  • Dedicated interview preparation, sharpening how you think, speak and present under pressure
  • A boardroom-style graduation panel with new faces who will challenge you on your work, just like a real interview
  • Dedicated mentor support from practitioners already working in your target field
  • Personal guidance throughout, you are never left to figure it out alone
  • Your defence is your graduation. The standard of your work earns your badge.
